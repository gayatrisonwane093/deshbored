Navbar examples

This example is a quick exercise to illustrate how the navbar and its contents work. Some
navbars extend the width of the viewport, others are confined within a .container. For
positioning of navbars, checkout the top and fixed top examples.

At the smallest breakpoint, the collapse plugin is used to hide the links and show a menu
button to toggle the collapsed content.
